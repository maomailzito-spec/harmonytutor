Teaching Ecosystem

Optimized teacher-student workflow

Harmony Tutor digitizes assignment exchange and technical correction, allowing lessons to focus on aesthetic and interpretive aspects.

Assignment Management

Harmony Tutor files are fully editable and streamline teaching communication:

  • 01. Assignment: Teachers can send files containing figured bass or given melodies. Students work out the harmonization in the editor and see real-time harmonic analysis as they enter notes.
  • 02. Exchange: Native files can be shared via email or institutional platforms (Google Classroom, Moodle). Students without the software can still interact by exporting and importing in MusicXML format.
  • 03. Review & Grading: Teachers analyze submissions with an immediate report on technical violations. They can focus on harmonic interpretation, modify parts and add text notes for assessment.
